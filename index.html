Introduction of GTVC Gulbahar Peshawar

Government Technical & Vocational Centre (GTVC) Gulbahar Peshawar is one of the oldest and most prestigious technical training institutes in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. Established in 1942, the institute operates under the Technical Education and Vocational Training Authority (KP-TEVTA). Its main objective is to provide quality technical and vocational education to youth and equip them with practical skills required by local and international job markets. GTVC Gulbahar offers various market-oriented training programs in fields such as Information Technology, Electrical Technology, Automobile Technology, Dress Making, Plumbing, and other technical trades. The institute emphasizes hands-on training through well-equipped laboratories and workshops, enabling students to develop professional skills and improve their employment opportunities. The vision of GTVC Gulbahar is to contribute to the economic development of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa by producing skilled and competent workers who can meet the demands of modern industries and play a positive role in the socio-economic progress of Pakistan.

Education

Government Technical & Vocational Centre (GTVC) Boys Gulbahar, Peshawar offers technical and vocational education under KP-TEVTA. The institute provides skill-based training programs to help students gain employment and technical expertise.

Courses offered include:

• Computer Operator (NVC Level 2) – 6 Months
• AutoCAD (NVC Level 2) – 6 Months
• Industrial Electrician (NVC Level 3) – 6 Months
• General Electrician (NVC Level 2) – 6 Months
• Motorcycle Mechanic (NVC Level 2) – 6 Months
• Electrician Grade-III – 1 Year

The institute was established in 1942 and focuses on providing quality technical and vocational training to students in Peshawar and surrounding areas.

Goals

Goal / Mission of GTVC Boys Gulbahar, Peshawar

The goal of Government Technical & Vocational Centre (GTVC) Boys Gulbahar, Peshawar is to engage youth in demand-driven technical and vocational training that matches modern market needs and technologies. The institute aims to improve employability, develop skilled manpower for industry, and contribute to the socioeconomic development of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.

Vision:

To create a prosperous and economically stable Khyber Pakhtunkhwa by producing a competitive skilled workforce capable of meeting both local and international market demands.

Objectives:

• Provide relevant technical skills for industrial and economic development.
